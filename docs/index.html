About me

I am a Bioinforamtics PhD student in the laboratory of Dr. Bing Ren. My research primarily foucuses on developing new techniques and computational methods to understand gene regulation at the level of single cell. As a part of BRAIN Initiative Project, I am currently working on constructing a spatially resolved single-cell atlas of mouse brain.
